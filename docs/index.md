## Vertical Focus

### Oil & Gas — Alberta

Single EOS platform across HQ and remote field sites, with streaming telemetry to CloudVision for remote visibility. EOS 4.36.0F includes streaming telemetry improvements relevant to high-latency remote site deployments.

- Ruggedized switching variants available for harsh environments
- CVP 2024.3 EoSS is urgent — upgrade to 2026.1.0 resolves both EoSS and SA-0135

### Municipalities — AB · MB · SK

Campus in a Box modular bundles for municipal offices and multi-site networks. Available through Canoe Procurement Group for eligible public sector organizations.

Priority targets: Lethbridge, Grande Prairie, Prince Albert, Swift Current, Steinbach.

### K-12 Education — MB · SK · Alberta

CloudVision zero-touch provisioning reduces per-site deployment time for school districts managing dozens of campuses.

Priority targets: Pembina Trails SD, River East Transcona (MB); Lethbridge SD #51, Wolf Creek (AB); Saskatoon Public Schools (SK).
